Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Hi 👋

Welcome to Graphlet.

Graphlet is a simple Mermaid diagram editor that runs in your browser. We built it to be easy to use and respectful of your privacy.

Here’s how it works.

1. No Account Required for Core Use

You do not need an account to use Graphlet.

You can:

  • Open the editor
  • Create diagrams
  • Edit them
  • Export them

All without signing up.

If we introduce accounts in the future, they will be optional and used for additional features such as:

  • Cross-device syncing
  • Collaboration
  • Higher usage limits for server-powered features

Core editing will remain available without registration.

2. Where Your Diagrams Are Stored

Your diagrams are saved locally in your browser using:

  • localStorage
  • Other browser storage mechanisms

This means:

  • Your diagrams stay on your device
  • We do not store them on our servers
  • We cannot see your diagrams

If you clear your browser storage, your saved diagrams may be removed.

3. Analytics (Privacy-Friendly)

We use cookieless, privacy-friendly analytics to understand general usage patterns.

This helps us see:

  • How many people visit
  • Which pages are used most
  • Device type
  • General region

We do not:

  • Track individual users
  • Use tracking cookies
  • Build behavioral profiles
  • Sell data

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous.

4. Optional Accounts in the Future

In the future, Graphlet may introduce optional accounts to enable features like:

  • Syncing diagrams across devices
  • Collaboration

If you choose to create an account:

  • We will collect only the information necessary to provide those features
  • We will clearly explain what data is stored
  • We will update this policy

Creating an account will never be required for basic diagram creation and editing.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers for:

  • Hosting
  • Content delivery
  • Privacy-friendly analytics

These providers may process minimal technical information such as IP address and browser details to operate the website.

6. Data Security

Because Graphlet primarily runs in your browser, you control access through your device and browser security.

If cloud-based features are introduced in the future, we will implement appropriate safeguards to protect stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Graphlet is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If Graphlet evolves, this policy may be updated.
Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
